Ramen

Description

Ramen is known for being easy to prepare while tasting great.
It is a quick and easy way for people who do not want to cook but are looking for a food to enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Instant ramen packet
  • Egg
  • Scallion

Steps

  1. Get water in a pot and bring it to a boil
  2. Put ramen noodles into the water and let it cook
  3. Put the ramen seasoning into the pot
  4. When ramen is done cooking, add an egg, and chopped up scallion into the pot
  5. Let the egg cook and enjoy!
