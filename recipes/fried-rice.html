Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice originates in eastern asian countries.
It is often made with left over ingredients and rice with soy sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cold rice
  • Vegetable oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Eggs
  • Garlic
  • Scallions
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Peas
  • MSG

Steps

  1. Heat a wok and pour vegetable oil into the wok and let the oil heat up
  2. Pour the eggs and break them down into little pieces
  3. Get the chopped up garlic and scallions and cook them with the eggs
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and MSG
  5. Add the cold rice to the mixture and cook until rice is broken down and mixed with the eggs
  6. Pour the soy sauce along the edge of the pan and mix it in
  7. Serve on a plate or a bowl and enjoy!
